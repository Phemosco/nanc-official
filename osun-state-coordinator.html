PERSONAL INFORMATION

Yusuff Opeyemi Ajibolu (born 30th of May in the early 90's) he's a Nigerian activist, and a pulitician. He chairs National Association of Nigeria comrades, Osun Chapter

Early life

Childhood and family

Comr.Yusuff Opeyemi Ajibolu Born on 30th of May in the early 90's to the family of Alhaji and Hajia Ajibolu, (Ajibolu Dynasty) of Eesa Compound, Obaagun, Osun State.

Education

Marcarony has he's been fondly called believed that education is the key to success in life, holds National Certificate of Education from Osun State College of Education, Ila-Orangun, also Bachelor's in Education in Ekiti State University Ado Ekiti.
He does not rest on his oars, as a personality who has focus and courage for his future pursuit, armed with excellent required qualifications, he got admitted to the Department of Public Adminstration at the Premier University for his Second Degree.

Career

Early career

On venturing into students politics at OSSCE Ila Orangun, he had romance with all political stalwarts ranging from the SUG (Apex Union) down to the Departmental Representatives and to the glory of God, he has successfully headed and served ranging from the Departmental, Faculty, and Indigenous Level.
He has also extend his tentacle outside his campus by contesting at the State level, which he won the Secretary General Position of National Association of Nigeria Comrade of Osun State Axis _Currently one of the leader to reckon with in Osun State College of Education Ila Orangun.
Been envisioned to live for others by developing more and more people "Martin Luther King Jr" once said "An individual has not started living until he can deliver himself from the narrow confinement of individualism to a broader concern of humanity".
This touches the very depth of his bone marrow and captures his vision on mankind, which prompted him to embark on respective humanitarian associations to join.
He got acquainted with Rotary/Rotaract when we were told to "ENGAGE ROTARY CHANGE LIVES" through PP Yusuff Toheeb Adewale & PP Olaitan Maroof Omotayo in Ila Orangun CB, after been equipped with necessary informations by PADR Oyegoke Afeez Adeyinka. He was lured back to Rotaract by PP Adeyeye Alexandra Oluwatobiloba, and since then he has been a very active Member of the Organization.
His positively participation and contribution in the Organization also helped in adding feathers to his cap for he has handled various capacities, which the Key are, Club President, District Director Media and Publicity, Zonal Editor in Chief, Zonal Director Media & Publicity (2x), District Electoral Chair, Zonal Administrator.

Awards and honours

Yusuff Opeyemi has numerous Awards to his credit from the various groups, Organization, Clubs, and Association in recognition of his outstanding performance in various capacities he had served as a Retractor he has been awarded ranging from Club, Zone, and District level, he has also served in Various Committee within the Rotary Family, but the common one is PUBLICITY COMMITTEE Well travelled within Nigeria.
He has been being to 13 States of the Federal Republic of Nigeria Yusuff Opeyemi Ajibolu is a practicing politician of note, focused and charismatic through his humanitarian gestures, he has rendered himself to all and sundry irrespective of difference whatsoever.

Humanitaria

A man of substance, committed to the well-being of people and development of the ancient city of Obaagun, Osun State and Nigeria at large to the best of his capability.
